Mapping the problem

FGM- the Female Genital Mutilation Network - tracks these dangerous acts around the world. The project helps define how much occurs in various regions by placing them on a five-point scale that ranges from low to very high.

  • Very low: Up to 2.8 percent of women have undergone any form of FGM
  • Low: Between 2.9 and 11 percent of women have undergone of FGM
  • Medium: Between 11.2 and 31.9 percent of women have undergone
  • High: Between 32.2 and 45 percent of women have undergone
  • Very High: Up to 76.6 percent of women have undergone.
