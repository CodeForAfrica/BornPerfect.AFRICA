About Born Perfect

Female Genital Mutilation (FGM) is a violation of the human rights of girls and women, and yet more than 200 million alive today have been cut in 30 countries across Africa, the Middle East and Asia. The ‘Born Perfect’ project documents FGM in Africa to spread awareness so that the number is reduced to zero.
