Mapping the problem

Born Perfect tracks the dangerous acts of Female Genital Mutilation (FGM) across Africa. It shines a light on these practices happening in various regions, placing them on a five point scale ranging from very low to very high incidences of women who have undergone a form of FGM.
  • Very low: Up to 2.8 percent.
  • Low: Between 2.9 and 11 percent.
  • Medium: Between 11.2 and 31.9 percent.
  • High: Between 32.2 and 45 percent.
  • Very High: Up to 76.6 percent.
