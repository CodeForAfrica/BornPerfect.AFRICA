Born perfect is an anti-Female Genital Mutilation (FGM) project in Africa, which was created by a transnational team of designers, journalist and developers to document incidents.

The anti-FGM project was piloted in Nigeria,Kenya and Tanzania and showcases articles ,videos photos and audi relating to FGM. It also gives an opportunity for anyone to report cases and get help.

The United Nations has ruled FGM as a violation of human rights, yet an estimated 200-miilion girls and women alive today have been cut in 30 countries across Africa, the Middle East and Asia.

This project seeks to provide information and spread awareness to reduce the occurence to zero.
